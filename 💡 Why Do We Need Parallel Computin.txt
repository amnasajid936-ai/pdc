WHY LOGIC IS CONVERTED INTO MATH?
If the data is already encrypted, why do we have to fundamentally rewrite the computer code from conditional logic (IF/THEN) into complicated math formulas?

The reason is simple and critical: To prevent the untrusted cloud from spying on the pattern of the computation, which would otherwise leak secrets.
